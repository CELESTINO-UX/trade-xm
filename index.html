How accurate are the trading signals?
Our AI models have consistently achieved 92-96% accuracy in backtesting across multiple market conditions. Actual live trading results vary but our users report an average 40% improvement in their win rates. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
What currency pairs do you support?
We currently support EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, AUD/USD, USDCAD, and XAU/USD (Gold). These are the most liquid forex pairs with the best trading conditions. We're constantly working to add more pairs based on user demand.
How do I connect Trade XM to my MT5 platform?
After subscription, you'll receive an API key and our Expert Advisor file. Simply install the EA on your MT5 platform, enter your API key, and the signals will automatically execute trades according to your risk parameters. Our setup guide makes this process simple even for beginners.

Absolutely! We encourage all new users to start with a demo account. All our plans include a 7-day free trial, and you can connect Trade XM to your MT5 demo account to test the signals risk-free before trading with real money.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa for Kenyan customers and all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) for international customers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.
